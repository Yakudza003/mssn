OVERVIEW OF MSSN-ADUSTECH

The Muslim Students Society of Nigeria, Aliko Dangote University of Science and Technology Wudil Branch, popularly referred to as MSSN-ADUSTECH, aimed to bring all Muslims in closer union and inculcate in them the true Islamic spirit for the achievement of peace among the students.

The Society, for over decades, since the inauguration of the first Branch Executive Council members in May, 2001 of the then MSSN-KUT Branch; has become a united entity for all Muslim students and mind changer of many young ones, it continue to grow wax stronger through its activities. Thousands of students has became moral conscious, religious literate and academic exceptional thereby memorizing the Holy Qur’an, reciting its portions, mastering Ibadah, developing personality, first-rate graduates, and obtaining intellectual stability.

In ADUSTECH Wudil, the MSSN became the uniting point for all students in the university whose both males and females students give their maximum contribution towards the improvement of the society under various forums and committees operated under the umbrella of the society to help it achieve its goals and pre-determine objectives.
